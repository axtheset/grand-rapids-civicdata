City of Grand Rapids, MI - CivicData.com Visualization Examples

A collection of visualizations using CivicData.com open data platform and the Accela Civic Platform as the data source.

  • Dashboard - A collection of general permit analytics
  • Heatmap - Construction cost density for permits issued from 2013 to the present
  • Time Interval - A time lapse representation of the last 6 months of permits issued
  • Word Cloud - A look at the frequency of words used in permit descriptions in the last 12 months
